Who We Are

GeneX Lab is an independent laboratory supply group focused on the sourcing and structured distribution of research grade compounds.

We operate with an emphasis on continuity, batch consistency, and repeat availability, supporting partners who require dependable long-term supply rather than consumer level access.

Our model is built around bulk procurement, private distribution, and professional handling standards.

We work exclusively with experienced research professionals, laboratories, clinics, and distribution partners who understand appropriate handling, documentation, and procurement processes. Minimum order thresholds may apply.

All materials are supplied strictly for laboratory research and analytical purposes only. Not for human or veterinary use.

Frequently Asked Questions

General

What does GeneX Lab supply?

GeneX Lab provides laboratory grade research materials intended for analytical, development, and professional research environments. We operate as a supply partner rather than a consumer retail brand.

Do you sell to individuals or first-time buyers?

Our operation is structured around professional supply relationships. Access is reviewed on a case-by-case basis to ensure suitability.

How does access work?

Initial contact allows us to understand your requirements and operating environment. Where appropriate, current availability information can then be shared for reference.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

Yes. Minimum order thresholds may apply depending on the materials and supply requirements involved.

Do you have a public price list?

No. Availability and pricing are not published publicly and are shared privately once access has been confirmed.

How is availability determined?

Availability is based on current production cycles and supply continuity. We prioritise repeat and long-term supply arrangements wherever possible.

Are your materials for medical or human use?

No. All materials supplied by GeneX Lab are intended strictly for laboratory research and analytical purposes only and are not for human or veterinary use.

Can you advise on usage or applications?

We do not provide guidance on application or use. Our role is limited to supply and availability of research materials.

How do I get started?

To begin, please contact our team and briefly outline your operating environment and supply requirements. GeneX Lab reserves the right to decline access where requirements or operating conditions are not aligned with our supply model.

Shipping & Delivery

Do you ship internationally?

Yes we support shipping. Details are discussed privately after initial contact.

What is the typical dispatch and delivery timeframe?

Once an order is confirmed and prepared, typical international delivery timeframes range between 10–14 business days, depending on destination, carrier, and customs processing. Please note that delivery times are estimates and may vary due to external factors beyond our control.

Is tracking provided?

Shipment tracking information is provided following dispatch.

How are materials packaged?

All materials are packaged professionally and discreetly in accordance with standard laboratory shipping practices.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods do you accept?

Payments are processed securely through approved payment platforms. Payment details are provided following order confirmation.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds may be issued for orders that are incorrect or damaged upon arrival, provided the issue is reported within 48 hours of delivery. Claims are reviewed on a case by case basis in line with our processing and fulfilment standards.

Are refunds available once an order has been dispatched?

Once an order has been dispatched, refunds are not available except in cases of verified error or damage.

What happens if a shipment is delayed?

Delivery timelines are estimates and may be affected by customs processing or carrier delays. We work with fulfilment partners to monitor shipments and assist where possible.

Why isn't pricing or availability listed publicly?

GeneX Lab operates on a private availability basis rather than a public retail model. Availability and pricing are shared privately following an initial review of requirements.
